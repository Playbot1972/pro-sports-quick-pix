Privacy Policy

Pro Sports Win is a sports entertainment and prediction app. It does not take bets, process wagers, show sportsbook odds, link to sportsbooks, accept deposits, pay winnings, or guarantee outcomes.

Information We Collect

When you use the app, we may collect basic information needed to run your account and app experience, such as your name, email address, phone number if you choose text verification, authentication status, Pro entitlement status, access code redemption status, and free-pick usage.

How We Use Information

We use this information to verify accounts, restore access, manage subscriptions or trial access, prevent repeated free-pick use, provide support, and keep the app functioning reliably.

Payments

Payments are handled by Stripe. Pro Sports Win does not store your full payment card number. Stripe may process payment and billing information according to Stripe's own privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

The app may use services such as Firebase, Stripe, EmailJS, Resend, and public sports data providers. Those services may process technical, account, or delivery data according to their own policies.

Sports Data and Predictions

Sports picks, simulations, and player information are for entertainment only. They are not financial advice, gambling advice, betting lines, sportsbook odds, or guaranteed predictions.
