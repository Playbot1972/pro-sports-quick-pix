Responsible Gaming

This app is for entertainment only. If you choose to participate, set a budget before you start and never chase losses.

  • Only play if you are 21 or older and it is legal where you are.
  • Set time and money limits before you begin.
  • Do not wager money needed for bills, rent, or essentials.
  • Take breaks if betting stops feeling fun.
  • If gambling is causing stress or harm, seek help immediately.

If you or someone you know needs help, contact a licensed counseling or support service in your area.

U.S. helplines: Problem gambling: 1-800-GAMBLER (1-800-426-2537). Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: 988 (call or text).
